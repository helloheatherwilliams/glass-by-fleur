About Fleur

Welcome to Glass by Fleur, based in Tattenhall, Cheshire creating bespoke and simple fused glass art and gifts. Fleur hand crafts each individual piece using the raw materials to make beautiful, fused glass pieces.

Fleur has worked for more than 20 years in the financial services sector however has always had a creative itch needing to be scratched! Having always loved glass and bought many pieces over the years, decided rather than just purchasing beautiful items she wanted to learn how these pieces were created. Now more than 10 years on and having attended many courses, is now creating and producing items in her own right in her home studio.

Inspiration

Inspired by the beauty of nature and the interplay of light. Her pieces are a celebration of organic shapes and the fabulous colours found in glass.

Custom Commissions

In addition to her items in the shop on this website, Fleur also offers custom commissions, transforming your artistic vision into a tangible reality. Whether you're seeking a mesmerizing centerpiece for your home or a one-of-a-kind gift for a loved one, Fleur will work closely with you to create a bespoke piece.
