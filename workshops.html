Workshops with Glass by Fleur

Whether you're looking for a relaxed creative session, a mindful team-building activity or a deeper, hands-on glass experience, my fused glass workshops are designed to be welcoming, inspiring and confidence-building.

I deliver workshops at your workplace, venur or chosen space, and I also run regular in-depth sessions at Ditzy Rose Makery in Tattenhall. From short introductory sessions for larger groups to full-day immersive workshops, each experience is carefully planned, fully supported and suitable for complete beginners as well as those with creative experience.

Participants often leave feeling calm, proud of what they've made - and surprised by what they're capable of.

Intro Workshops - "Workshop in a Box"

These shorter sessions are ideal for larger groups, corporate wellbeing, team-building, parties and celebrations. Everything is brought to youm making them easy to organise and accessible for everyone.

All materials, tools and guidance are provided, and finished pieces are taken away to be fired and returned afterwards.

Prices start from £25 per person, depending on design and complexity.

In-depth Fused Glass Workshops

Longer, more immersive workshops offer time to relax into the process, build confidence and explore more complex designs and techniques. These sessions are calm, friendly and supportive, and work beautifully for complete beginners as well as those with creative experience.

From £50 per person for a 2-hour workshop.

Venues & Booking

I'm a mobile tutor and can deliver workshops at workplaces, community spaces or hired venues. A suitable space with tables, good lighting and room to work comfortably is important. If needed, I'm happy to help advise on or source a suitable venue.

I also run regular workshops at Ditzy Rose Makery in Tattenhall, where I'm a resident tutor.

Prices may vary depending on group size, venue and workshop content - I'm always happy to discuss options and tailor a session to suit your group.
